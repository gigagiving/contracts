Step 1:		Compile sol file(s)
Step 2:		Publish Token Contract (GigaGivingToken)
Step 3:		Publish ICO Contract (GigaGivingICO), with the account ID to fund encoded in the contract, and the account address of the Giga Coin token contract
            as a constructor parameter.
Step 4: 	Transfer 12,000,000 Giga Coins to the ICO contract.
Step 5: 	Transfer 3,000,000 Giga Coins to the funding target account.
Step 6:		User Accounts send Ether to the ICO Contract address, and are given Giga Coins in return on a phase based scale.
Step 7: 	5 Weeks after start time (Nov. 15) - contract concludes.
Step 8:     Funding Target account calls safeWithdrawal on the ICO contract, and receives the Ethereum balance; and any remaining tokens.
